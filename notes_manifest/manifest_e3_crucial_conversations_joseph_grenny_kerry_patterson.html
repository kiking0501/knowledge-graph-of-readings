3. Principle: Choose Your Topic

  • Skill
    • Unbundle, Choose, Simplify
    • Content, Process/Pattern, Relationship (CPR)
  • Crucial Question
    • What's the right topic to address to move toward what I really want?
    • Is this a Content, Pattern/Process, or Relationship Issue?

4. Principle: Start with Heart

  • Skill
    • Focus on what you really want
  • Crucial Question
    • What am I acting like I really want?
    • What do I really want? - For me? For others? For the relationships?
    • What should I do right now to move toward what I really want?

5. Principle: Master My Stories

  • Skill
    • Retrace my Path to Action
    • Separate Fact from story
    • Watch for Three Clever Stories
    • Tell the Rest of the Story
  • Crucial Question
    • How am I behaving? What am I feeling? What story is creating those feelings?
    • Get back to the facts: What have I heard or seen that supports my story? Are there other facts that challenge my story?
    • Am I telling Victim, Villain, or Helpless Stories?
    • What am I pretending not to know about my role in the problem?
    • Why would a reasonable, rational, and decent person do this?
    • What should I do right now to move toward what I really want?

6. Principle: Learn to Look

  • Skill
    • Look for when the conversation becomes crucial
    • Look for safety problems
    • Look for your own Style Under Stress
  • Crucial Question
    • Am I going to silence or violence?
    • Are Others?

7. Principle: Make It Safe

  • Skill
    • Apologize when appropriate
    • Contrast to fix or prevent misunderstanding
    • CRIB to get Mutual Purpose (Commit to seek, Recognize the purpose behind the strategy, Invent a mutual purpose, brainstorm new strategies)
  • Crucial Question
    • Why is safety at risk? -- Have I established Mutual Purpose? Am I maintaining Mutual Respect?
    • What will I do to rebuild safety?

8. Principle: STATE My Path

  • Skill
    • S: Share your facts
    • T: Tell your story
    • A: Ask for others' paths
    • T: Talk tentatively
    • E: Encourage testing
  • Crucial Question
    • Am I really open to others' views?
    • Am I talking about the real issue?
    • Am I confidently expressing my own views?

9. Principle: Explore Others' Paths

  • Skill
    • Ask
    • Mirror
    • Paraphrase
    • Prime
    • Agree
    • Build
    • Compare
  • Crucial Question
    • Am I actively exploring others' views?
    • Am I avoiding unnecessary disagreement?

10. Principle: Retake Your Pen

  • Skill
    • Collect
    • Understand
    • Recover
    • Engage
  • Crucial Question
    • What must I do to make myself feel safe?
    • What must I do to affirm my own worth?

11. Move to Action

  • Skill
    • Decide how you'll decide
    • Document decisions and follow up
  • Crucial Question
    • How will we make decisions?
    • Who will do what by when?
    • How will we follow up?
