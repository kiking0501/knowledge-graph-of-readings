序言 Introduction
1. 人生的基本矛盾 The Fundamental Contradiction of Human Life
2. 自古以來人類就認識到生命的基本矛盾。人類的啟蒙者向人們揭示了能解決這種內在矛盾的生命的定義，但偽善者卻對人們掩飾它 The Contradiction of Life has been recognized by Men since remote antiquity. The Enlighteners of Humanity have revealed to Men the definitions of life, which solve this Internal Contradiction, but the Pharisees and the Scribes conceal them from Men
3. 學究們的迷誤 The Delusions of the Scribes
4. 學究們的理論以人的肉體存在的外表現象來偷換人的整個生命的概念，並由此得出關於人生目的的結論 The Doctrine of the Scribes substitutes the visible phenomena of his animal existence for the concept of the whole life of Man, and from these makes gis deductions as to the aim of his life
5. 偽善者和學究們的錯誤理論既不能解釋真正的生命的意義，也不能作為生活的指南；生活的唯一指南是不能用理性來解釋的生命的慣性 The false teachings of the Pharisees and of the Scribes do not give any explanations of the meanings of actual life, nor any guidance in It; as the only guide of life there appears the inertia of life, which has no rational explanation
6. 我們人類意識的分歧 The Doubling of the Consciousness in the Men of our world
7. 意識的分裂是由於把動物的生命與人的生命相混淆而產生的 The Doubling of the Consciousness is due to confusing the Animal Life with the Human Life
8. 分裂和矛盾是沒有的，它只存在於錯誤的學說中 There is no doubling and no contradiction: they appear only with the False Teaching
9. 人的真正的生命的誕生 The Birth of the True Life in Man
10. 理性就是被人領悟了的法則，人的生命應當遵循這一法則 Reason is that law cognized by Man, by which his life is to be accomplished
11. 知識的錯誤方向 The False Direction of Knowledge
12. 錯誤的知識產生的原因是把對象放在錯誤的背景上 The Cause of the False Knowledge is the False Perspective in which objects present themselves
13. 事物的可知性增加了，不是由於事物在時間和空間上顯現出來，是而由於支配我們和我們所研究的事物的法則是統一的 The Knowableness of Objects does not increase in consequence of their manifestation in Space and Time, but in consequence of the Unity of the Law to which we and all the objects which we study are subject
14. 人的真正的生命不是那種在時間和空間中產生的東西 Man’s true life is not what takes place in Space and Time
15. 拋棄動物性的幸福是人的生命的法則 The Renunciation of the Good of Animal Personality is the Law of Human Life
16. 動物性是生命的手段 The Animal Personality is an Instrument of Life
17. 精神的再生 Birth by the Spirit
18. 理性意識需求甚麼 The Demands of the Rational Consciousness
19. 對理性意識的需求之肯定 The Confirmation of the Demands of the Rational Consciousness
20. 人的個體的需求與理性意識的需求互不相容 The Demand of Personality seems incompatible with the Demand of the Rational Consciousness
21. 不要求根本棄絕個體的需求，而要求使它服從理性 What is demanded is not a Renunciation of Personality, but its Subjection to the Rational Consciousness
22. 愛的感情是服從於理性意識的個體活動的表現 The Sentiment of Love is the manifestation of the activity of Personality subjected to the Rational Consciousness
23. 不懂得自己生命意義的人不可能有愛的表現 The Manifestation of the Feeling of Love is impossible for Men who do not understand the meaning of their life
24. 真正的愛是棄絕個體幸福的結果 True Love is the consequence of the Renunciation of the Good of Personality
25. 愛是真正的生命唯一充實的活動 Love is the only full activity of the True Life
26. 人們為改善自己的生存所做的種種徒勞的努力，做他們不可能獲得唯一真正的生命 The Endeavours of Men, directed upon the impossible improvement of their existence, deprive them of the possibility of their only, True Life
27. 對死亡的恐懼只不過是對無法解決的生命矛盾的意識
28. 肉體的死亡消滅了屬於空間的軀體和屬於時間的意識，但不能消滅構成生命基礎的東西－－ 每一個人對待世界的特殊關係 The Carnal Death destroys the Spatial Body and the Temporal Consciousness, but cannot destroy what forms the Foundation of Life, the Special Relation which each being bears to the world
29. 對死亡的恐懼之所以產生是因為人們把被他們的錯誤相似所局限的生命的一部分當成了整個生命 The Terror of Death is due to this, that Men regard as their life ene small part of it, which is limited by their own False Conception of it
30. 生命是對世界的態度。生命的運動是確立新的、最高的態度，因此死亡是進入新的態度。 Life is a Relation to the World. The Motion of Life is the establishment of a new, higher Relation, and so Death is the Entrance into a new Relation
31. 死亡的生命在這個世界上沒有終止 The Life of dead people does not cease in this world
32. 對死亡的迷信之所以產生是由於人把自己與世界的各種不同關係混淆起來了 The Superstition of Death is due to this, that Man confuses his different relations to the world
33. 可以看到的生命是生命的無限運動的一部分 The Visible Life is a part of the Infinite Motion of Life
34. 塵世生活的痛苦無法解釋雄辨地對人證明，人的生命並不是始於生而終於死的個體生命 The Inexplicability of the Sufferings of the Earthly Existence proves more convincingly than anything else to Man that his Life is not a Life of the Personality, which began with Birth and ends with Death
35. 肉體的痛苦是人的生命和幸福的必要條件 Physical Sufferings form the Necessary Condition of the Life and Good of Man
總結 Conclusion
